XR · Technical Art · 3D · Procedural Modelling & VFX

Where VFX meets immersive worlds.

I am Murad, an XR developer and technical artist with a background in computer graphics and computer science. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Augmented and Virtual Reality Design at Hochschule Darmstadt, focusing on real-time XR development, procedural modelling and interactive experience design.

Before moving to Germany I completed a Bachelor's in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science at Baku State University and worked as a 3D generalist at ROG VFX, contributing to the historical TV series Tagiev for national television in Azerbaijan. My work spans VR/AR development, technical art, procedural modelling in Houdini and real-time rendering, always with a focus on performance and visual quality.

Based in Darmstadt, Germany · Open to internships & junior roles
